Who We Are

Building India's Smartest Retail Supply Chain

Ecoo Basket Retail Supply Chain Pvt. Ltd. is a next-generation FMCG distribution company headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu. We provide end-to-end supply chain solutions that bridge the gap between manufacturers and retailers with unmatched efficiency.

Our technology-first approach integrates AI-driven logistics, advanced warehouse management systems, and digital procurement platforms to ensure seamless product flow from factory to shelf.

With a robust network of warehouses, a growing fleet, and a passionate team, we are committed to becoming India's most trusted retail supply chain partner.
