Your Growth is Our Mission

At Ecoo Basket, we believe that business partnerships should be built on a foundation of shared growth, mutual trust, and transparent collaboration. Partner Benefits

What You Gain as Our Partner

When you join the Ecoo Basket partner ecosystem, you unlock a comprehensive suite of advantages designed to accelerate your growth and strengthen your market position.

Distribution Network

Immediate access to our established distribution network covering Chennai and connecting your products to supermarkets, grocery stores, hotels, restaurants, bakeries, and institutions.

Fast Delivery

AI-optimized route planning and our expanding fleet ensure your products reach retail shelves within 24–48 hours of order confirmation, every time.

Warehouse Capacity

Our modern, strategically located warehouse facility features climate control, WMS integration, and real-time inventory visibility — keeping your products safe and accessible.

Digital Procurement

Our cloud-based procurement platform enables seamless digital ordering, automated invoicing, GST compliance, and real-time order tracking with complete transparency.

Market Intelligence

Gain access to data-driven market insights on consumer demand patterns, seasonal trends, regional preferences, and competitive dynamics to sharpen your go-to-market strategy.

Brand Visibility

Retail marketing support, shelf placement optimization, point-of-sale material management, and promotional campaign execution to maximize your brand's retail presence.

Long-Term Partnership

We are committed to building lasting, mutually profitable partnerships. Dedicated account managers, quarterly business reviews, and growth planning sessions are standard for every partner.
