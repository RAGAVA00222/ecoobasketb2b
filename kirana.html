Problem & Fix

What usually goes wrong — and our actual answer.

ISSUE 01

Delivery windows nobody keeps

Salesmen promise a day, the truck shows up whenever. Our fix: route-planned delivery with a WhatsApp update if something's delayed — not silence.

ISSUE 02

Minimum orders too high for a small shop

You're forced to over-stock just to qualify for an order. Our fix: flexible order sizes that match what you actually sell.

ISSUE 03

Messy or missing GST invoices

Handwritten slips that cause problems at filing time. Our fix: a clean, GST-compliant invoice on every single order.

ISSUE 04

A different salesman every time

No one who actually knows your shop or remembers last week's issue. Our fix: one direct account contact per partner, order after order.

ISSUE 05

Reordering means chasing someone by phone

Calling around, hoping someone picks up. Our fix: reorder through our digital ordering platform whenever you need to.

ISSUE 06

Damage or shortage claims go nowhere

You report a problem and never hear back. Our fix: claims are tracked and followed up on directly by your account contact.
