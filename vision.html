The Road Ahead

Where we're headed — and how we plan to get there.

This is a roadmap, not a promise. We'd rather show you the plan honestly than dress it up as done.

Our Roadmap

Four phases, built in order.

PHASE 01

Digital Foundation

Get the basics right: digital ordering, GST-compliant invoicing, and reliable delivery across our current Chennai coverage — the phase we're in now.

PHASE 02

Zone-by-Zone Expansion

Grow our Chennai footprint area by area, adding retailers without letting delivery reliability slip.

PHASE 03

Tamil Nadu Coverage

Extend distribution beyond Chennai to other Tamil Nadu markets, using what we learn locally first.

PHASE 04

Category & Partner Growth

Deepen the own-brand Nuts & Spices line and grow our supplier partner base as the network matures.

Where We Won't Cut Corners

Growth on our terms, not at the expense of reliability.

A

Delivery Consistency

We won't add new areas faster than we can serve them reliably.

B

Honest Reporting

Partners and retailers get accurate numbers, not optimistic rounding.

C

Compliance First

GST, licensing and regulatory basics stay current before we chase new markets.
