Our Story

A Vision Born to Transform Retail Distribution

Ecoo Basket Retail Supply Chain Pvt. Ltd. was founded with a singular bold vision — to revolutionize FMCG distribution across India by making it smarter, faster, and more accessible. Headquartered in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, we have built a foundation rooted in technology, trust, and an unwavering commitment to our partners.

We recognized a critical gap in India's retail ecosystem: manufacturers struggled to reach the right retailers efficiently, while retailers lacked access to quality products at fair prices. Ecoo Basket was built to bridge this divide through a technology-driven, data-informed distribution model that benefits every stakeholder in the supply chain.

From our state-of-the-art warehouses to our AI-powered logistics platform, every aspect of our operation is designed to deliver excellence — ensuring that the right product reaches the right retailer at the right time, every time.
