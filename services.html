What We Offer

Comprehensive Solutions Powering Retail Growth

From procurement to last-mile delivery, our integrated suite of services covers every dimension of the modern supply chain — tailored to meet the dynamic needs of India's retail ecosystem.

Retail Distribution

Multi-channel retail distribution across urban and rural India. We deliver direct to store with optimized routes, route mapping, and coverage expansion capabilities for leading FMCG brands. Our dedicated field force ensures maximum shelf presence and retailer satisfaction.

Wholesale Supply

Bulk procurement and wholesale distribution with highly competitive pricing structures. We serve kirana stores, general trade, and modern trade outlets with flexible order quantities, credit facilities, and reliable delivery schedules that keep their shelves stocked.

Warehouse Management

State-of-the-art warehousing with warehouse management system (WMS) integration, climate-controlled zones, CCTV monitoring, and optimized storage layouts. Our facilities ensure product safety, traceability, and rapid dispatch for all FMCG categories.

Inventory Management

AI-powered automated inventory tracking delivers real-time visibility across all stock locations. Intelligent demand forecasting, stock optimization algorithms, and proactive replenishment systems ensure zero stockouts and eliminate overstock situations.

Supply Chain Solutions

End-to-end supply chain management orchestrates the seamless flow of products from manufacturer to retailer. Our intelligent route planning, multi-modal logistics, and last-mile delivery systems reduce costs and improve speed at every touchpoint.

Procurement Services

Strategic sourcing, vendor identification, and procurement management ensure the best products at the most competitive prices. Our team handles vendor negotiations, quality audits, compliance verification, and contract management on your behalf.

Business Consulting

Expert advisory services for retail and FMCG businesses seeking growth. We provide data-driven insights on market expansion, category management, distribution strategy, and operational efficiency improvements tailored to your unique business needs.

Private Label Support

End-to-end private label development including product sourcing, formulation support, packaging design, quality testing, regulatory compliance, and retail placement. We help businesses create distinctive private label products that compete effectively.

FMCG Distribution

Specialized FMCG distribution expertise with cold chain capabilities for perishables, rigorous expiry date management, FIFO inventory practices, and real-time product tracking from warehouse to final delivery point across the distribution network.

AI & Technology

Harnessing artificial intelligence for smarter supply chain decisions. Our AI platform delivers accurate demand prediction, route optimization, digital ordering capabilities, and comprehensive real-time analytics dashboards for data-driven business growth.
