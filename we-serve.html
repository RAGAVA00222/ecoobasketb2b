Our Reach

Serving Every Corner of the Retail Ecosystem

Ecoo Basket's expansive distribution network empowers a diverse spectrum of businesses — from neighbourhood kirana stores to large-format hypermarkets, from healthcare institutions to government organizations. Wherever there is a need for quality FMCG products delivered reliably and efficiently, Ecoo Basket is there.

Sectors

13 Sectors We Empower

A comprehensive overview of the industries and business types that rely on Ecoo Basket for their FMCG supply chain needs.

Retail Stores

Kirana shops, convenience stores, and general trade outlets — served with regular doorstep delivery, flexible order sizes, and competitive pricing that helps small retailers thrive.

Supermarkets

Modern trade supermarket chains supplied with bulk volumes, structured category planning, promotional support, and shelf management expertise for maximum product visibility.

Hypermarkets

Large-format retail chains managed with high-volume supply, competitive trade pricing, in-store promotional campaigns, and dedicated account management for smooth operations.

Hospitals

Healthcare institutions supplied with essential FMCG products, hygiene items, patient care consumables, and pantry essentials — with compliance-focused procurement and reliable delivery.

Hotels

Hospitality industry partners supplied with premium quality food & beverage products, personal care amenities, and housekeeping essentials from trusted national and international brands.

Restaurants

Quick service restaurants, casual dining, and fine dining establishments supplied with food ingredients, condiments, packaging materials, and kitchen essentials at the best prices.

Catering

Catering businesses and large-scale food service providers supplied with bulk food products, disposables, serving ware, and event-based procurement solutions for any scale of operations.

Corporate Offices

Enterprise and SME offices supplied with pantry essentials, premium beverages, stationery, hygiene products, and all workplace FMCG needs through scheduled, hassle-free delivery.

Educational Institutions

Schools, colleges, and universities supplied with canteen food products, stationery, hygiene and cleaning supplies, ensuring students and staff have everything they need.

Government Organizations

Government departments, PSUs, and public sector offices served with fully compliant procurement processes, transparent supply chains, and GST-compliant documentation.

Manufacturers

Manufacturing partners supported with raw material sourcing, packaging material distribution, logistics coordination, and last-mile delivery to production facilities.

Distributors

Sub-distribution partnerships that expand our collective market reach into regional and micro-markets, enabling greater penetration for manufacturers and better service for retailers.

Export Partners

International trade facilitation bringing premium Indian FMCG products to global markets — with quality documentation, export compliance, and reliable international logistics support.

Personal Care
